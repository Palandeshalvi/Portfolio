Breathe with Me!

Designing and Evaluating Wearable Visual and Vibrotactile Displays for Colocated Breathing Synchronisation.

Project Duration February, 2021 to May, 2022 Master's Thesis Project
Design Type New Media Design, Wearable Tech, Interactive Installation
Tools & Skills Android, Python, R Studio, Figma User Research, System design, Usability Testing, Analysis
Find out more! UTwente Thesis Repository

Humans are social creatures and by nature, we have a sophisticated system to connect with each other. Breathing synchronisation is the key to understanding each other's emotions, state of mind and even intent. And what's great is that it can be done consciously as well as sub-consciously. What if we could harness the power of wearable technology to share each other's breath and get to know each other a little bit better?

Here is an attempt to understand how visual and vibrotactile displays can be used to showcase our breathing patterns to loved ones. And which one is better to get our breaths synchronised over time? Read on to understand how I explored about this concept.
